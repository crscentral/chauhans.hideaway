Our Story

A Legacy of Celebration in the Heart of Rajasthan

✦ ✦ ✦

Born from the proud Chauhan heritage — a lineage that has long stood as a symbol of valour, honour, and royal tradition — The Chauhans Hideaway is more than a venue. It is an estate where history breathes through every detail, where the golden spirit of Rajasthan becomes the canvas for your most extraordinary celebrations.

Nestled in the serene landscape of Devliya, Banar, on the outskirts of Jodhpur, our sprawling open-air estate offers the rare gift of space: vast sandy grounds beneath a boundless Rajasthan sky, punctuated by ancient trees that have witnessed seasons turn for generations.

Every event at The Chauhans Hideaway is treated with the reverence it deserves. From the first enquiry to the final farewell, our team is dedicated to crafting memories that endure long after the marigolds have been swept away.

Heritage & Lineage

Our Leadership Legacy

Our leadership is not merely one of modern enterprise; it is the continuation of a 1,500-year-old royal bloodline defined by unyielding resilience, honor, and an indomitable spirit. Our roots trace back to the foundations of Hindustan, beginning with Shri Vasudev Singh Ji Chauhan in 551 AD and Samrat Prithviraj Singh Ji Chauhan III. Retrace our journey from the historic stand at Tarain, the migration to Siwan, and the founding of Ghorgahiya, down to our Owner and Director, Shri Ravindra Singh Ji Chauhan, and our current generation, Shri Sumant Singh Chauhan and Shri Sourav Singh Chauhan.

The Venue

Where the Desert Sky Becomes Your Canopy

The estate at Devliya spreads across generous open land, dotted with native trees that cast dappled shadows across golden earth. Under Jodhpur's legendary sky — cerulean by day, star-strewn by night — it becomes a stage unlike any other. Bring your vision; we provide the horizon.

About the Land

A Canvas of Sand, Sky and Ancient Trees

✦ ✦ ✦

The Chauhans Hideaway estate is a study in raw Rajasthani beauty. Sparse, sculptural trees rise from warm ochre soil — their forms silhouetted against skies that shift from brilliant blue to amber and rose as the day unfolds. Surrounded by lush green borders and open to the horizon on all sides, this land carries the quiet grandeur that has long made the Thar region one of India's most evocative landscapes.

The venue's wide-open character makes it equally suited to intimate garden ceremonies and grand marquee celebrations. There are no walls to confine your imagination — only the earth, the sky, and the gentle whisper of wind through the canopy above.
